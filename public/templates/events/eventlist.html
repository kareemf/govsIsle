Alchemy Creamery

Alchemy Creamery, started by a few lactose intolerant friends in a cramped NYC apartment, creates unique flavor combinations fused with hazelnut, almond and coconut milk blends. Their creations are dairy-free, gluten-free, as well as certified kosher and vegan.

Located in Liggett Terrace Food Court.
Open Everyday.
